# Research Depth And Budget

Choose the smallest depth that can answer safely.

| Depth | Use when | Typical budget | Minimum evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| light | One narrow fact, command, API parameter, or release check | 5-15 minutes | One primary source or one direct local observation |
| standard | Comparison, planning input, test strategy, PRD reference, open-source selection | 20-60 minutes | Multiple source types, conflicts checked |
| deep | Security, compliance, purchase-impacting decisions, disputed facts, or long-lived standards | 1-3 hours or explicit budget | Primary sources, independent corroboration, conflict handling |

Stop when:

- key claims are supported or explicitly marked unsupported;
- new searches only repeat known sources;
- conflicts are documented;
- deadline or user budget is reached;
- access restrictions block further reliable collection.

Do not use fixed numbers such as "always 3 rounds" or "always 3 sources for high confidence". Use claim risk and evidence fit.
